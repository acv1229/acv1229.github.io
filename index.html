01 — Research Paper
NYU · Dec 2025
Distribution-Aware Companding Quantization of LLMs (DACQ)

Novel post-training quantization framework for LLMs. Showed transformer weights follow logistic distributions across 192 LLaMA and 168 Qwen submodules. Applied CDF-based companding quantization, consistently reducing weight reconstruction error vs. AWQ baseline. Achieved 19% throughput gain over FP16 on Llama3-8B. Evaluated on NYU A100 GPUs.

02 — Research Paper
NYU Langone · Dec 2025
Transformers for Survival Analysis (TabPFN Fine-tuning)

Investigated whether TabPFN generalizes to healthcare time-to-event prediction. Generated 10,000 synthetic survival datasets spanning diverse hazard functions and censoring regimes. Fine-tuned via meta-learning, improving probabilistic calibration (Integrated Brier Score) in low-data regimes. Benchmarked against CoxPH, RSF, DeepSurv, DeepHit, SurvTRACE on METABRIC and SUPPORT2.

Self-Supervised Learning for Robust Image Classification

Deep Learning final project under Professors Yann LeCun and Alfredo Canziani. Compared DINO vs. iBOT self-supervised vision transformers under 500k–1M training image constraints.
